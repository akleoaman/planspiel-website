About Us

Robo-Werk is a startup company created by students of TU Chemnitz. In this era of digital transformation, yet, there are many businesses needed user interaction to complete the similar task repeatedly. For this type of task, Robo-Werk is specialized in providing solutions with Robotic Process Automation to replace the repetitive efforts of users by automation. The main focus of Robo-Werk is to provide services that needs less human interaction and more self-execution.

Our Mission

Robo-Werk is born to reimagine the repetitive processes with pioneer ideas. It thinks of automation to meet the customer's satisfaction and elevate customer's experience. To enhance efficiency, exponentially cut down the costs & make work more inspiring, Robo-Werk always brainstorms to continuously improve and deliver excellence in solution. It introduces the human society to a much happier place to work and live.

Our Purpose

Our company places customers at the core in every business decision make. Customer satisfaction, cost reduction, productivity, availability, reliability and performance matter a lot. So, our indefatigable team work 24x7 to overcome customers obstacles with a balanced automation. Speed increases in multiple folds. Quite often quality is inherited and preserved and sometimes upgraded.

Our purpose is a mere rational improvement - perceivable, measurable and noticeable.

Our Values

Our values include "commitment to customers, discipline, risk-taking and constant improvement." They are the mainstream to everything we do.

Our commitment defines the responses of employees to challenges our customers face in the society. Our organizational discipline shows the speeds of teamwork and utilize the information and experiences gained by individual workers to develop better policies and program and drive to goal. That is why the company has no fear of taking risk to come out with new innovations. We push the limits, break the barriers and make thing happen with constant improvement.

Our Vision

Robo-Werk will be recognized as a best service provider and the foremost choice of customers in the field of Automation by 2021.
